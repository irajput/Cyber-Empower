Teach/Mentor

How can I get involved?

You can be either an instructor or mentor for the camp. More information is listed below as well as the eligibility criteria

What does it mean to be an instructor?

As an instructor you will present the daily lecture and teach the core programming topics. In addition, you will help students by answering their questions as they work on their projects, exercises, and programs.

What does it mean to be a mentor?

As a mentor you will assist the instructor in answering any questions students might have. You will also be helping them as they work on their projects

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for an instructor position you must be a current sophomore in high school or older. To be eligible for the mentor position, you must be a current freshman in high school or older. It is required that instructors have a strong knowledge of Python and that mentors are very familiar with the language. Exceptions to this will be made on a case by case basis. Please email cyberempowercamp@gmail.com for questions regarding eligibility.
